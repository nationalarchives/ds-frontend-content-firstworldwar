Map of the Battle of Jutland
Map of the Battle of Jutland, 31 May-1 June 1916.

This map shows the positions and movements of the British and German naval fleets at the Battle of Jutland during the late afternoon and early evening of 31 May 1916. It is possible to discern the positions of the three Royal Navy battle cruisers - Indefatigable, Queen Mary ('Queen M') and Invincible - that were sunk during the battle, as well as the position of Lützow, the only German battle cruiser to go down).
Catalogue reference: MFQ 1/3664 (c. 1916-20)
