J R R Tolkien: trench fever
Extract from the service record of J R R Tolkien: form confirming Tolkien's return to England from France due to trench fever, 22 November 1916; letter from Tolkien to the War Office declaring himself fit to report for duty, 2 January 1917.
Catalogue reference: WO 339/34423 (22 Nov 1916, 2 Jan 1917)
