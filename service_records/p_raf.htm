RAF muster roll
Extract from the Royal Air Force (RAF) muster roll, April 1918.

This muster roll listed in service number ('Air Force No.') order every serviceman enlisted with the RAF at the time of its creation on 1 April 1918. It gives information about an individual's 'trade classification' (i.e. his job in the RAF) and rank; date and terms of enlistment; rate of pay. All of the men listed in this extract originally joined either the Royal Flying Corps (RFC) or the Royal Naval Air Service (RNAS) - the two organisations that were amalgamated as the RAF in the spring of 1918.
Catalogue reference: AIR 10/237 Part 2 (Apr 1918)
